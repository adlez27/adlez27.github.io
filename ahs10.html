AHS Collaboration Album

Celebrating the 10th anniversary of Kiyoteru, Yuki, and Miki's release

On December 4th, 2009, Artist House Software released the Vocaloid 2 products Hiyama Kiyoteru, Kaai Yuki, and SF-A2 codename miki. Ten years later, creators from all over the world will converge to celebrate the impact that these Vocaloids have made. An album of all-new original music and music videos will be produced for this occasion.

Fill out the signup form by June 30 to join! You may choose to sign up with existing collaborators, or be randomly matched up with other creators. A single person may take on multiple roles, or even work on multiple songs. Please consider the workload you can handle over the next six months.


Sign up starting June 7ate9

Just like KiyoteruQuest and SENSEITIONAL, this album will be distributed worldwide on Spotify, iTunes, Bandcamp, and distributed in Japan on dozens of domestic music platforms. After the distributor and AHS take their cut, all of the earnings will go back to every person involved. Please ensure that the software and materials you plan to use are licensed for commercial use.


music_note
Musician

Musicians will be composing instrumental tracks and mixing the final version of the song. Mastering of the whole album together will be handled by DystoP.

Since tasks are split up into multiple roles, you can join even if you don't own any AHS vocaloids.

format_align_left
Lyricist

Lyricists will write lyrics and compose a melody to go with the instrumental track being composed by a musician.

Although these vocaloids were only made to sing in Japanese, lyrics can be written in any language you want, as long as you collaborate with the tuner to achieve the right result.

computer
Tuner

Tuners will tune and render Kiyoteru in Vocaloid.

You must have purchased at least one of these voicebanks: Kiyoteru V2, Kiyoteru V4 Rock, Kiyoteru V4 Natural, Yuki V2, Yuki V4, Miki V2, Miki V4.

We want to invite users of any skill level, but experience with English lyrics will help a lot!

create
Artist

Artists will create track artwork for each song and artwork assets for music videos, such as characters and backgrounds.

movie
Video Editor

Video editors/animators will put the final music video together using the song and artwork assets.
