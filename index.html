You may remain in the queue indefinitely, and cancel at any time before work on your commission begins. You may be queued for several months. You will be notified every time you move up one place in the queue. Once you reach the #1 position, you will have one week to respond before being automatically removed.

Once work begins on your commission, you can check the progress by coming back to this page. If you would like to stop partway through, you can either cancel the commission entirely, or receive partially completed work for the appropriate price.
