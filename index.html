Commissions

You may remain in the queue indefinitely, and cancel at any time before work on your commission begins. You will be notified once you reach the top place in the queue for your commission type, and you'll have 2 weeks to respond before being automatically removed.

Once work begins on your commission, you'll be updated on the progress once a week, or as often as requested. If you would like to stop partway through, you can either cancel the commission entirely, or receive the partially completed section for the appropriate price.
