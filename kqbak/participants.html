KiyoteruQuest

A Hiyama Kiyoteru collaboration album for Open House 2019

Every year on April 10th, we're invited to visit Kiyoteru's elementary school. For the fandom, this is another opportunity to celebrate Kiyoteru. So on April 10, 2019, we plan to release a full album crossfade, and on each day after will be a music video for each of the songs.

These are the creators that will be working on this album. Please look forward to their work!
